The Purpose

The purpose of this website is to delve into the rich histories of some popular programming languages.

We'll be focusing on languages like Python, C, and JavaScript.

But before we dive into that, let's understand why programming languages are worth exploring.

Why Programming Languages Matter

  • Programming languages are the building blocks of the digital world.
  • They enable us to communicate with computers and instruct them to perform tasks.
  • Each language has a unique history, purpose, and impact on the tech industry.
